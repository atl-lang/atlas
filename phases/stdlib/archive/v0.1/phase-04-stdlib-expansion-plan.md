# Phase 04 - Stdlib Expansion Plan

## Objective
Define a controlled roadmap for stdlib expansion beyond v0.1.

## Inputs
- `docs/api/stdlib.md`

## Deliverables
- List of v1.0 stdlib modules (e.g., `io`, `path`, `json`, `time`).
- Criteria for adding new stdlib functions.

## Steps
- Identify core modules to add post v0.1.
- Document inclusion criteria and stability policy.

## Exit Criteria
- Expansion plan accepted and referenced in docs.
