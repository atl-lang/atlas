# Phase 06 - Parser Recovery Strategy

## Objective
Define how the parser handles and reports errors consistently.

## Inputs
- `docs/diagnostics.md`

## Deliverables
- Policy on early-exit vs minimal recovery.
- Tests that validate error reporting behavior.

## Steps
- Decide recovery behavior for mismatched braces and missing tokens.
- Add tests for recovery scenarios.

## Exit Criteria
- Parser recovery policy documented and enforced.
